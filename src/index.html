Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q.

    How many times do I need to do a full body health check?

    Generally, you should do a full body check at least once a year.

    However, there are different checkup packages that apply to you depending on your age range.

    If you have specific risk factors or depending on the outcome of your first checkup, it may be neccessary to do a second check, or a selected number of tests at regular intervals.

  • Q.

    Why do I need health checkup when I am not sick or experiencing symptoms?

    Many dangerous diseases or disorders may start silently and develop over time inside your body without showing external symptoms.

    You need regular health checkups to capture these diseases on time and tackle them before they develop into serious problems.

  • Q.

    What happens if I miss my monthly payment?

    We encourage you to be up to date with your payments to enjoy the full benefits of your subscription.

    However, if you miss your payment, You can still access your package provided you settle the missed payment in the following month.

    Missing your payment for up to three (3) consecutive months will mean forfieting all your previous contributions.

  • Q.

    For how long will my package remain valid?

    Your packages remain valid for one year. They are designed such that you will have done all the tests in your package by the end of the year.

    Test Packages are renewable on a yearly basis.

  • Q.

    Will LabDx Health prescribe drugs for me?

    LabDx Health is focused on your total health care. We will normally link you with our partner specialists for medical consultation where your test results indicate the need for that.
